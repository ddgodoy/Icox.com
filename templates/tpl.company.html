Corporate Information

Mission

Icox Consulting is a professional services firm specialised in the design and execution of integral solutions orientated to B2B, B2C, B2E models inter alia. Icox Consulting focalizes in consulting, positioning, e-commerce, back end and front end developments for E-business and E-Government based systems.
Icox Consulting´s services aim to help organisations in their “profitability” revenue, productivity, growth and competitiveness requirements through the assessment in custom strategies, information and communication technologies development and implementation, which meet the digital economy conditions.

History

Icox Consulting was born in 1995 as a Binational company in Córdoba, Argentina, and Montreal, Canada, working from its beginning in the emerging internet economy. During its existence, our firm has acquired great expertise in the subject, growing not only in knowledge, but also in development, innovation and implementation of the best technologies in the Internet field for Industries such as Tourism, Telecommunications, Financial Services, Customer and Industrial Market, Government and Natural resources, And Editorial.
During 2001, Icox Consulting associates with “Sitio Digital”, a Web Architecture development vanguard company, aiming to offer the best quality services to our clients.
Besides the main services that define and distinguish our firm, we are currently working on the implementation of new P2P technology based solutions in Canada, and on offshore developments for international companies in Argentina.
