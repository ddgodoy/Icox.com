Quality Assurance

Today, information systems have become one of the most critical parts of companies; consequently, application errors pose a significant risk to their results. Similarly, the increased demand for adaptations in these applications is causing some companies to see how the daily maintenance and development of new projects cause great instability in their systems which leads to a poor perception of their services by the customer areas.
Icox knows how important quality control is becoming in the IT processes of organizations and the factorization potential that this type of services involves, so it has therefore decided to prioritize this aspect in its portfolio.

Main functions of the QA service

The test run requires the coordination of several steps, many testers, and a long series of scripts in different versions of the application code which are integrated. Solving this complexity requires having solutions that enable visibility of the entire cycle from a single point of control.
